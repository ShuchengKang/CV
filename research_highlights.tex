    \section{\mysidestyle Research Highlights}

    \textbf{Fast and Certifiable Trajectory Optimization} 

    \vquad A powerful framework to compute \textit{certifiably optimal solutions} for \textit{nonconvex trajectory optimization} problems defined by polynomial objectives and constraints. 
    
    \vquad The \textit{first GPU-based} large-scale SDP solver implemented in C++ and CUDA. Achieves up to \textit{$10\times$ speed-up} compared to existing solvers and runs in real time when combined with data-driven warm-starts.
        
    \vquad Accepted by \textit{WAFR'24}. \href{https://arxiv.org/pdf/2406.05846}{[Paper]} \href{https://computationalrobotics.seas.harvard.edu/project-strom/}{[Website]}

    \textbf{Sparse Polynomial Optimization with Unbounded Sets}

    \vquad Extends sparse Moment-SOS Hierarchy to \textit{unbounded sets with homogenization techiques}. New asymptotic convergence guarautees are established.
        
    \vquad Submitted to \textit{SIAM Journal on Optimization}. \href{https://arxiv.org/pdf/2401.15837}{[Paper]}

    \textbf{Verification and Synthesis of Robust Control Barrier Functions}

    \vquad Provides a \textit{multi-level polynomial optimization framework} for robust CBF verification and synthesis. Asymptotic global convergence to optimality is guaranteed.

    \vquad Accepted by \textit{CDC'23}. \href{https://arxiv.org/pdf/2303.10081}{[Paper]}
