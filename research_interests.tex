    % Research Interests
    \section{\mysidestyle Research\\Interests}

    Optimization; Robotics; Control \\[2mm]
    I aim to provide both \textit{theoretical foundations} and \textit{computational algorithms} for the next generation of decision making systems. \\[2mm]
    Currently, I focus on bridging nonconvex optimization and convex optimization in fundamental problems such as trajectory optimization. I do so by (a) leveraging the moment and sums-of-squares (SOS) hierarchy to derive tight convex semidefinite programming (SDP) relaxations of the nonconvex problems, and (b) developing novel SDP solvers with unprecedented scalability that build upon new theory of large-scale convex optimization, exploit the problem-specific sparsity structure, and harness modern computing hardware such as GPUs.
